Scene Graph genetation

A Scene Graph, is a structured representation of an 3D scene, where nodes in a Scene Graph correspond to object bounding boxes with their object categories, and edges correspond to their pairwise relationships between objects.

Details

Prediction of object-postions

We used heatmaps to predict potential positions for objects in the scene. Based on position-data from scenes handmade in the earlier experiment, a heatmap for each piece of furniture is created and later processed appropriate to the instruction of where to put the object.
